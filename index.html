Ingredients needed for Churros


  • 1 Cup Water
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 quarts of frying oil

Directions


  1. over medium heat, combine water, sugar, salt, and vegetable oil
  2. heat oil for frying
  3. Fry until golden
  4. drain on paper towels
